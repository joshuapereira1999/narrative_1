Grammar

berraying(a)
English: this (non-singular)
Class: demonstrative
Berrayinga daloong ngoojalentha.
These daloong are green.
ngayang/ngayany
English: my
Class: adjective
Ngenjayinga ngayang mayaroong.
This is my house.
ngelayiny(a)
English: this (fem)
Class: demonstrative
Ngelayinya gawooleng.
This is a woman.
ngenjaying(a)
English: this (masc)
Class: demonstrative
Ngenjayinga jawaleng.
This is a man.
thena?
English: what (thing)?
Class: interrogative
Thena berrayinga?
What is this?
